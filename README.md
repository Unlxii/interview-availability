# Interview Availability — Ronnachai Sitthichoksathit

**Live page:** https://unlxii.github.io/interview-availability/

A quick way for recruiters and hiring teams to schedule an interview with me without back-and-forth emails.

## How to use this page

1. **Check my availability** — the calendar shows the next 4 weeks. Green = available, red = busy (exam or commitment). You can also pick any specific date in the "Check a specific date" box for an instant answer.
2. **Open a ticket** — fill in the short form (company, position, proposed time). Clicking **Send via email** opens a pre-filled email to me — nothing is stored on this site.
3. **Request my resume** — tick "Request my resume" in the form. I verify the company and reply by email, usually the same day.
4. **Stay updated** — tick "Notify me if this availability changes" and I'll email you if my schedule moves.

## Good to know

- Timezone: **Asia/Bangkok (GMT+7)** · preferred hours Mon–Fri 09:00–18:00, weekends on request.
- I reply within a few hours on weekdays. Corporate email addresses get priority.
- The calendar updates automatically — what you see is current.
- This page uses no cookies and no tracking.
